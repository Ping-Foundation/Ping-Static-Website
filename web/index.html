Event-Brief

Ping Foundation organized 10 days free Kotlin Android Workshop from 16th July to 27th July. 30 participants attended the event, which included students, developers, and working professionals. The event was organized in association with IAMAI Mobile 10X at Govt. Cyberpark Calicut.

The sessions was handled by Nikhil Kilivayil and Praful Dev. Along with the technical session, startup interaction was also there. Attendees were rewarded with certificates.
