Kerala Blockchain Hub

KERALA BLOCKCHAIN HUB

Collaborate all the Blockchain related activities

Kerala Blockchain Hub is an initiative by Ping Foundation in order to collaborate all the Blockchain related activities. A Platform to bring developers, professionals, experts and academicians together and build Blockchain community to host Blockchain related events. Kozhikode District Collector Sriram Sambasiva Rao launched Kerala Blockchain Hub on 5th January 2019 by launching Kerala Blockchain Hub website (https://kbhub.org) . The inauguration ceremony was followed by Blockchain Summit, where Blockchain related sessions and discussions were conducted. First session on Blockchain was handled by Srinivas Mahankali who is the head of Blockchain Business Unit, ULTS and author of the book ‘Blockchain The Untold Story’. The session was followed by discussion of different use cases of Blockchain in supply chain, handled by Sanat Bhatt, Senior Blockchain architect (ULTS).
